# Customer Support & Success — Strategy & Task List

## Goal: Deliver outstanding client experience that generates referrals

---

### Onboarding

- [ ] Write a "Welcome to Kloudbiz" onboarding message to send every new client on day 1
- [ ] Create a simple client intake form (Google Form) to collect all info needed before work starts: brand colours, logo, copy, goals
- [ ] Set clear expectations upfront: timeline, number of revisions, what you need from the client and when
- [ ] Book a kick-off call (even 15 min) for every new project to align before starting

### Communication Standards

- [ ] Respond to every client message within 4 business hours (set this as your SLA)
- [ ] Send a weekly progress update for any project running longer than 1 week
- [ ] Use WhatsApp for quick updates, email for formal quotes and documents
- [ ] Never go silent — if there's a delay, proactively communicate it before the client asks

### Delivery & Handoff

- [ ] Deliver every project with a written handoff summary: what was built, how to use it, what's next
- [ ] Record a short Loom or screen share walkthrough for any website or app handoff
- [ ] Include a "what's included for 30 days after delivery" support window in every quote
- [ ] Ask for formal sign-off or approval in writing (WhatsApp or email) on every completed project

### Retention & Satisfaction

- [ ] Send a "30-day check-in" message after every project delivery
- [ ] Track Net Promoter Score — after each project, ask: "How likely are you to recommend us? (1–10)"
- [ ] Build a client directory in your CRM with renewal/upsell dates flagged
- [ ] Create a loyalty offer for returning clients (e.g. 10% off second project)

### Reviews & Referrals

- [ ] Ask every satisfied client for a Google review — send them the direct link
- [ ] Ask every satisfied client: "Do you know another business that could use our help?"
- [ ] Showcase testimonials on kloudbiz.com within 48 hours of receiving them
- [ ] Acknowledge and thank every review publicly (reply on Google)
