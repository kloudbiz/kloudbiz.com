# Sales — Strategy & Task List

## Goal: Sign the first 3 paying clients within 90 days

---

### Phase 1 — Foundation (Week 1–2)

- [ ] Define your 3 target client profiles (e.g. local restaurant, Indian-diaspora business, new tradie startup)
- [ ] Write a one-paragraph pitch for each service (website, Google Business, app, AI tools)
- [ ] Create a simple quote/pricing sheet (Starter / Growth / Premium tiers per service)
- [ ] Set up a free CRM — use Notion, HubSpot free, or a Google Sheet with columns: Name, Contact, Service Interest, Status, Follow-up Date
- [ ] Build an email/WhatsApp message template for first outreach

### Phase 2 — Lead Generation (Week 2–4)

- [ ] List 20 local Melbourne businesses that need a better web presence (search Google Maps for businesses with no website or poor listings)
- [ ] Reach out to 5 people in your personal network who own or manage a small business
- [ ] Join 2 local Melbourne business Facebook groups or LinkedIn groups and introduce yourself
- [ ] Post your first "I help Melbourne businesses get online" LinkedIn post with your kloudbiz.com link
- [ ] Ask 3 friends or family members for a warm referral or intro

### Phase 3 — Pipeline & Closing (Week 3–8)

- [ ] Send first batch of 10 outreach messages (WhatsApp or email) using your template
- [ ] Follow up every non-reply after 3 days, then again after 7 days
- [ ] Book at least 2 discovery calls (30 min via Google Meet or phone)
- [ ] Send a written quote within 24 hours of every discovery call
- [ ] Close your first paid project — even a small one ($200–$500) to build momentum and a case study
- [ ] Log every interaction in your CRM same day

### Phase 4 — Retention & Upsell

- [ ] Send a "30-day check-in" message to every delivered client
- [ ] Offer a maintenance or content retainer after project delivery
- [ ] Ask every happy client for a Google review and a referral
- [ ] Track your close rate (quotes sent vs. accepted) — aim for 30%+
