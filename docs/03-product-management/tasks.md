# Product Management — Strategy & Task List

## Goal: Define clear, sellable service products with repeatable delivery

---

### Service Definition

- [ ] Document every service Kloudbiz offers with a clear name, scope, and outcome (website, Google Business, app, AI tools, content)
- [ ] For each service, define: what's included, what's NOT included, timeline, and price
- [ ] Package services into tiers: Starter, Growth, Premium — with upsell paths between them
- [ ] Create a one-page service menu PDF clients can review before a discovery call

### Roadmap

- [ ] List the top 3 services most likely to generate revenue in the next 90 days
- [ ] Rank all services by: ease of delivery vs. client value vs. price point
- [ ] Identify which service to lead with as your "loss leader" or low-friction entry offer
- [ ] Plan Q3 2026 and Q4 2026 service roadmap (what new offerings to add)

### Customer Validation

- [ ] Talk to 5 potential clients to validate which problems they most want solved
- [ ] Ask each: "What's the biggest challenge your business has online right now?"
- [ ] Document recurring themes and use them to refine your service descriptions and website copy
- [ ] Test a new service concept as a pilot with one client before fully launching

### Delivery & Releases

- [ ] Create a delivery checklist for each service (what steps happen, in what order, with what handoffs)
- [ ] Define what "done" looks like for each service (acceptance criteria the client signs off on)
- [ ] Set up a simple project tracker (Trello, Notion, or Linear) for active client work
- [ ] After each project, run a 15-min retrospective: what went well, what to improve
