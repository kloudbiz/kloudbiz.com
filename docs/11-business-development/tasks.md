# Business Development — Strategy & Task List

## Goal: Build partnerships and open new revenue channels beyond direct client work

---

### Partnership Opportunities

- [ ] Identify 5 complementary businesses to partner with (accountants, bookkeepers, real estate agents, photographers, business coaches)
- [ ] Reach out to 2 partners per month with a simple value-exchange proposal (referral fee or reciprocal referrals)
- [ ] Create a one-page "Partner Program" document explaining what Kloudbiz offers and what partners earn per referral
- [ ] Formalise referral fee: e.g. 10–15% of first project value, paid after client pays

### Channel Sales

- [ ] List yourself as a recommended digital partner in at least 2 Melbourne business associations or networks
- [ ] Explore listing Kloudbiz on freelancer marketplaces (Airtasker, Upwork, PeoplePerHour) as an additional inbound channel
- [ ] Build a relationship with 1 web hosting reseller program (e.g. SiteGround, Kinsta) for a recurring commission on client hosting

### New Markets

- [ ] Assess whether the Indian-diaspora business community in Melbourne is an underserved niche (you already have multilingual site support)
- [ ] Explore whether any of your services could be delivered remotely to clients outside Melbourne (other Australian cities, New Zealand)
- [ ] Research whether there is demand for AI training/consulting from Melbourne SMBs — your background positions you well

### Strategic Alliances

- [ ] Approach 1 local digital marketing agency about white-labelling your development services
- [ ] Explore co-hosting a free workshop or webinar with a complementary business (e.g. "How to get your business online in 2026")
- [ ] Look into Google Partners program eligibility as your Google Business service grows

### Pipeline Tracking

- [ ] Add a "BD / Partnerships" tab to your CRM to track all partner conversations separately from client leads
- [ ] Review BD pipeline monthly — which partnerships are generating referrals, which are stale
- [ ] Set a target: at least 1 referral lead per month from partner channels within 6 months
