# Executive Leadership & Strategy — Strategy & Task List

## Goal: Run Kloudbiz with clear direction, measurable goals, and a 12-month vision

---

### Vision & Mission

- [ ] Write a 1-sentence mission statement: what Kloudbiz does, for whom, and why
- [ ] Write a 3-year vision: where you want Kloudbiz to be (revenue, team size, services, reputation)
- [ ] Define your "unfair advantage" — what makes Kloudbiz different from every other Melbourne web studio
- [ ] Revisit and refine these annually — not quarterly

### Goals & KPIs

- [ ] Set 3-month goals (e.g. first 3 clients, $5,000 AUD revenue, 10 LinkedIn posts)
- [ ] Set 12-month goals (e.g. $60,000 AUD revenue, 20 clients delivered, 1 retainer client)
- [ ] Define 5 KPIs to track monthly: new leads, proposals sent, close rate, revenue, client satisfaction score
- [ ] Build a simple monthly dashboard (Google Sheets) to track all KPIs in one place

### Decision-Making

- [ ] When evaluating new services or opportunities, ask: does this align with our target client, can we deliver it well, does it generate enough margin?
- [ ] Use the "3 questions before saying yes" rule: Is it profitable? Is it strategic? Do I have capacity?
- [ ] Document major business decisions and the reasoning behind them (a simple journal or Notion log is enough)
- [ ] Review big decisions at the end of each quarter — what worked, what you'd do differently

### Fundraising & Capital (Future)

- [ ] At this stage, bootstrap — keep costs minimal, grow from revenue
- [ ] If you need capital to scale, explore: LaunchVic grants (Victorian startup grants), business loans, or angel investment
- [ ] Research Australian startup ecosystem resources (LaunchVic, StartupVic, Stone & Chalk)
- [ ] Do not raise capital until you have a proven, repeatable revenue model

### Quarterly Business Review

- [ ] Block 2 hours at the end of every quarter for a solo business review
- [ ] Review: revenue vs. target, clients won and lost, what services sold best, what to stop/start/continue
- [ ] Update your 12-month goals based on what you learn
- [ ] Celebrate wins — acknowledge milestones, no matter how small

### Accountability

- [ ] Find a peer accountability partner (another founder or freelancer) to meet with monthly
- [ ] Consider joining a founder community or mastermind group in Melbourne
- [ ] Share your quarterly goals with one trusted person who will hold you to them
