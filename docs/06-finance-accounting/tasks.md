# Finance & Accounting — Strategy & Task List

## Goal: Track every dollar, invoice on time, and stay compliant as an Australian business

---

### Setup

- [ ] Register Kloudbiz as an Australian business — confirm ABN is active (check ABR.gov.au)
- [ ] Open a dedicated business bank account (separate from personal) if not already done
- [ ] Choose accounting software: Xero (recommended for AU), MYOB, or Wave (free)
- [ ] Set up a Stripe or Square account for accepting card payments from clients
- [ ] Register for GST if annual turnover is expected to exceed $75,000 AUD (or register voluntarily)

### Pricing & Revenue

- [ ] Set your minimum viable project price (floor below which you won't take work)
- [ ] Calculate your effective hourly rate target: desired monthly income ÷ billable hours
- [ ] Define payment terms: e.g. 50% upfront, 50% on delivery
- [ ] Decide if you'll charge in fixed-price or hourly — fixed is easier to sell, hourly is safer for scope-creep risk
- [ ] Review and update pricing every 6 months

### Invoicing

- [ ] Create a professional invoice template with ABN, payment terms, and bank details
- [ ] Send invoices within 24 hours of project completion or milestone reached
- [ ] Set up automated payment reminders for overdue invoices (7 days, 14 days)
- [ ] Keep a record of every invoice issued, paid, and outstanding

### Budgeting & Cash Flow

- [ ] Map your monthly business expenses (domain, hosting, tools, software subscriptions)
- [ ] Set a monthly revenue target and track actuals vs. target weekly
- [ ] Maintain a 3-month cash runway in your business account at all times
- [ ] Review P&L monthly — even a simple spreadsheet is enough at this stage

### Tax & Compliance

- [ ] Lodge BAS (Business Activity Statement) quarterly if registered for GST
- [ ] Set aside 25–30% of every invoice payment for tax (income tax + GST)
- [ ] Keep receipts for all business expenses (software, equipment, internet, phone)
- [ ] Book a session with an Australian accountant before end of financial year (30 June)
